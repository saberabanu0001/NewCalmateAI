Quick Well-being Tips

Practice Gratitude

Take a moment each day to reflect on three things you're grateful for.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to maintain physical and mental well-being.

Take Breaks

Regular breaks help reduce stress and improve focus and productivity.

Connect with Nature

Spending time outdoors can significantly improve mood and reduce stress.

Need Immediate Support?

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, help is available 24/7.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 or 1-800-273-8255

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

Emergency Services: 911
